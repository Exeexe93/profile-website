Easy Claim

Easy Claim is developed during my first year summer holiday as a school project, Orbital. It allows user to save copy of taxi receipt via taking photo or screenshot. It is capable to convert image of taxi receipt to relevant data such as date, time, taxi fare and etc by using Google ML Kit api. Furthermore, this project uses Google Firebase for storing the users' data such as images of the taxi claims and information.
